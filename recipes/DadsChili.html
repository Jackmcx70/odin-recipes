Dad's Chile

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground meat
  • 2 cans light red kidney beans
  • 3 cans stewed tomatoes
  • 1 can chili beans
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 3 tbsp worcestershire
  • 3 tsp chili powder

Instructions

  1. Brown Meat and drain grease
  2. Add green pepper and onion, tomatoes, worcestershire sauce and chili powder
  3. Cook for 1 hour then add beans and cook for another half hour
