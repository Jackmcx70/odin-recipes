Coffee Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1-1/4 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla

  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • 1-1/4 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1/3 cup of melted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350deg and lightly grease 9 X 13 dish or pan
  2. In large mixing bowl, mix eggs, oil, milk and vanilla
  3. In a medium bowl, blend flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  4. Add dry ingredients to the wet and mix well, pour half the batter into the baking dish
  5. In a medium bowl combine brown sugar and cinnamon and chopped nuts and mix
  6. sprinkle half of the cinnamon mixture on top of the first layer of batter and top with remaining batter
  7. top off with remaining cinnamon sugar, drizzle melted butter over the top
  8. Bake for 35-40 minutes
