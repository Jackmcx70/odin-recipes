Hamburger Steak And Gravy

Ingredients:

Hamburger Steaks

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/3 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 tsp seasoned salt
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder

Onion Gravy

  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth

Instructions:

Hamburger Steaks

  1. Place all ingredients into a bowl and mix well. Mixing with hands is best
  2. Form mixture into patties
  3. Spray a heavy skillet with nonstick cooking spray
  4. Brown each patty on a medium high heat for 3 minutes per side
  5. Remove from pan and set aside

Onion Gravy

  1. Add onions to the same pan. Add a little extra oil in necessary
  2. Saute until onions start to brown
  3. Add butter and let it melt
  4. After butter melts, whisk in the flour until no longer visible
  5. cook, whisking constantly for 1 minute
  6. Whisk in the beef broth until no flour lumps
  7. Bring to a simmer, whisking until thickened
  8. Add the beef patties back into the gravy
  9. Set heat to medium-low, cover, and allow it to simmer 20-30 minutes or until patties reach 160deg in the center
  10. Remover from heat, cover, and let stand 5-10 minutes
